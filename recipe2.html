Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Spices (garam masala, turmeric, paprika)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 can tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cream

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices for 1 hour.
  2. Grill or pan-fry chicken until cooked.
  3. Sauté onions, add tomato puree and cream.
  4. Combine sauce and chicken, simmer for 10 mins.
  5. Serve with rice or naan.
