Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta until crisp.
  3. Whisk eggs and cheese in a bowl.
  4. Drain pasta and mix quickly with egg mixture and pancetta.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then serve immediately.
